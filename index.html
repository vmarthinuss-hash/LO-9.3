Reading Strategist's Handbook

1. Skimming

Reading rapidly to get the general overview. Focus on titles, subheadings, and the first/last sentences of paragraphs.

2. Scanning

Looking for specific information like dates, names, or numbers. Don't read everything; move your eyes in a Z-pattern.

3. Intensive Reading

Deep analysis of logic and grammar. Used to understand complex themes and the connection between points.

4. Critical Reading

Evaluating the author's tone, bias, and intent. Asking: "Why did they write this and what is the underlying message?"
